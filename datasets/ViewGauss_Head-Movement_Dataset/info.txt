Name: ViewGauss Head-Movement Dataset
Citation: L. Zhang, A. Cameron, K. Chug, et al. ViewGauss: A dataset for 6DoF head movement during free viewing of 4D Gaussian videos. arXiv preprint arXiv:2401.05492, 2024.

Sources:
 - https://github.com/Cedarleigh/ViewGauss-DataSet
 - https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.05492

Notes:
35 participants watched 4 sequences of Gaussian-splatting videos; dataset includes 6DoF head motion (position and quaternion).

Download Instructions:
Download the zipped GitHub repository to obtain CSV files for each participant; each file records Frame, Position (PosX, PosY, PosZ) and Quaternion (RotX, RotY, RotZ, RotW) sampled at 10 Hz.
