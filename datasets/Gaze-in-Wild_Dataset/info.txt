Name: Gaze-in-Wild Dataset
Citation: R. Kothari, P. Mital, and J. Henderson. Gaze-in-wild: A dataset of eye and head movements during natural tasks. Scientific Reports 10, 2020.

Sources:
 - http://www.cis.rit.edu/~rsk3900/gaze-in-wild/
 - https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-020-77276-z

Notes:
19 participants performed navigation, ball catching, search and tea-making tasks; includes head orientation velocities and gaze data.

Download Instructions:
Navigate to the project website; download the compressed data files and code. Dataset includes head pose (orientation) and eye gaze signals with IMU and video.
