Name: EyeNavGS 6-DoF Navigation Dataset
Citation: H. Ren, C. Yang, S.-H. Chen, et al. EyeNavGS: A 6-DoF Navigation Dataset and Record-n-Replay Software for Real-World 3DGS Scenes in VR. arXiv preprint arXiv:2403.06001, 2024.

Sources:
 - https://symmru.github.io/EyeNavGS/
 - https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.06001

Notes:
46 participants explored 12 3D Gaussian Splatting scenes; dataset includes head pose, gaze, view index, FOV and timestamp per frame.

Download Instructions:
Download the zipped GitHub repositories for the Rutgers and NTHU collections. Each archive contains scene folders with CSV files that record per-frame head position (X,Y,Z) and quaternion orientation, gaze data, and timestamps.
