Name: 360-degree Saliency Dataset (PanoSaliency)
Citation: Anh Nguyen, Zhisheng Yan, and Klara Nahrstedt. Your attention is unique: Detecting 360-degree video saliency in head-mounted display for head movement prediction. In Proceedings of the 26th ACM international conference on Multimedia, pages 1190–1198, 2018.

Sources:
 - https://zenodo.org/record/2641282
 - https://github.com/phananh1010/PanoSaliency

Notes:
Provides over 50,000 saliency maps for 24 360° videos; raw head movement traces obtained from other datasets.

Download Instructions:
Download the saliency_ds.zip file from the Zenodo record; it contains saliency maps derived from head movement datasets.
