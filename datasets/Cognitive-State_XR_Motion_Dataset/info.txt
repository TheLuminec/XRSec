Name: Cognitive-State XR Motion Dataset
Citation: Kaiang Wen, et al. Understanding cognitive states from head and hand motion data. arXiv preprint arXiv:2309.12507, 2025.

Sources:
 - https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.12507

Notes:
Collects head, left-hand and right-hand positions and quaternions at 72 Hz with frame-level cognitive state annotations (reading, confusion, hesitation, readiness).

Download Instructions:
The dataset (head and hand motion with cognitive-state labels) will be released with the final publication. Monitor the project page for updates.
