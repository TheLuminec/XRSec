Name: Panonut360 Dataset
Citation: Y. Xu, J. Gutiérrez, P. Le Callet. Panonut360: Head and eye tracking dataset for 360-degree videos. 2024.

Sources:
 - https://dianvrlab.github.io/Panonut360/
 - https://dianvrlab.github.io/Panonut360/download.html

Notes:
50 users watched panoramic videos; dataset totals >60 hours of head and eye tracking data.

Download Instructions:
Use the Google Drive folder to download the dataset and video segments. The DataProfile folder contains CSV files with head position, head orientation (quaternion) and eye gaze sampled at 120 Hz.
