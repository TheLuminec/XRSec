Name: NJIT 6DOF VR Navigation Dataset
Citation: J. Chakareski. 6DOF VR navigation dataset, 2019. (Data collection described on the project page.)

Sources:
 - https://web.njit.edu/~chakarsk/vr-navigation.html

Notes:
Comprises 30,000 samples per session of head position and orientation captured at 250 Hz as users navigated an indoor arena.

Download Instructions:
Contact the dataset authors (Prof. Jakov Chakareski) to obtain the 6DOF navigation dataset; includes x,y,z positions and yaw,pitch,roll orientations at 250 Hz.
