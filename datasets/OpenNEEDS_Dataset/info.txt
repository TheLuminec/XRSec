Name: OpenNEEDS Dataset
Citation: E. Sun, K. Muhlbach, P. Zhang, et al. OpenNEEDS: An open, large-scale dataset of head, hand and eye motion for VR interaction. 2021.

Sources:
 - https://research.facebook.com/datasets/openneeds/
 - https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-022-00605-y

Notes:
Participants performed reading, drawing, shooting and object manipulation; dataset captures non-eye and eye data.

Download Instructions:
Visit the Meta research page; sign a data-use agreement to obtain the dataset. It includes head, hand, scene and gaze data for 44 participants across various tasks.
