Name: CREATTIVE3D Dataset
Citation: R. Martins, M. Mestre, et al. CREATTIVE3D: A dataset of VR road-crossing interactions under normal and low-vision conditions. Scientific Data 11, 2024.

Sources:
 - https://zenodo.org/records/14514163
 - https://zenodo.org/record/8269108
 - https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-024-03382-2

Notes:
40 participants explore road crossings in VR; dataset includes head position and rotation at 125 Hz and gaze orientation.

Download Instructions:
Download the Data.zip and related archives (Scenarios.zip, Tools.zip, Usage examples.zip, questionnaire_translation_EN.csv) from the Zenodo record. Data.zip contains the main dataset with head position, head rotation, gaze vectors, physiological signals and event markers recorded at 125 Hz.
