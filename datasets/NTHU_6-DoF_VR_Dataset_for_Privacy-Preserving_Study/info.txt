Name: NTHU 6-DoF VR Dataset for Privacy-Preserving Study
Citation: Yu Wei, Chun-Ying Huang, Kuan-Ta Chen, et al. A 6-DoF VR dataset of 3D virtual world for privacy-preserving approach and utility-privacy tradeoff. In ACM Multimedia Systems (MMSys) 2023.

Sources:
 - https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3593712.3593793

Notes:
Collected for studying privacy in VR; includes 6DoF head and controller data and gaze signals.

Download Instructions:
Contact the authors of the MMSys 2023 paper to request the dataset; it contains head and controller positions, orientations, gaze, audio and text features for 6-DoF VR sessions.
