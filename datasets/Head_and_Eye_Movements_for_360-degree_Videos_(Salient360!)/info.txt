Name: Head and Eye Movements for 360-degree Videos (Salient360!)
Citation: Erwan J. David, Jesús Gutiérrez, Antoine Coutrot, Matthieu Perreira Da Silva, and Patrick Le Callet. A dataset of head and eye movements for 360 videos. In Proceedings of the 9th ACM Multimedia Systems Conference (MMSys), pages 432–437, 2018.

Sources:
 - https://zenodo.org/record/10650505
 - https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3204949.3208161

Notes:
Also known as the Salient360! dataset; includes head and eye tracking for 19 videos and ground-truth saliency maps.

Download Instructions:
Download the 7z archives from the Zenodo record; they contain head-only, head+eye, and stimuli data for 360° videos.
