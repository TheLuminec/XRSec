Name: Ruhr Hand Motion Catalog
Citation: V. Burger, S. Babula, F. Hürlimann, et al. Hand motion catalog of human center-out transport trajectories. Scientific Data 10, 2023.

Sources:
 - https://osf.io/x4bpy/
 - https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-023-02688-1

Notes:
Although focused on hand trajectories, the dataset also includes head IMU/Vicon marker positions and rotations in each frame.

Download Instructions:
Download the archive (Ruhr_HandMotion_CenterOutTask.tar.gz) from OSF; contains measurement and processed CSVs with positions and rotation quaternions for head markers and sensors.
