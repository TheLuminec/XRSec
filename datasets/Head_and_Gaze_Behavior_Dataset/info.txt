Name: Head and Gaze Behavior Dataset
Citation: Yili Jin, Junhua Liu, Fangxin Wang, and Shuguang Cui. Where are you looking? A large-scale dataset of head and gaze behavior for 360-degree videos and a pilot study. In Proceedings of the 30th ACM International Conference on Multimedia, pages 1025–1034, 2022.

Sources:
 - https://cuhksz-inml.github.io/head_gaze_dataset/
 - https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3503161.3548360

Notes:
Contains nearly 20 million frames of head and gaze tracking data from 27 360° video clips.

Download Instructions:
Use the Google Drive folder (or Aliyun link) to download the dataset and video segments. Each zip file contains head and gaze traces for multiple videos; sign-in may be required.
