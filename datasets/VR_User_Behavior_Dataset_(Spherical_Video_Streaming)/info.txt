Name: VR User Behavior Dataset (Spherical Video Streaming)
Citation: Chenglei Wu, Zhihao Tan, Zhi Wang, and Shiqiang Yang. A dataset for exploring user behaviors in VR spherical video streaming. In Proceedings of the 8th International Conference on Multimedia Systems, MMSys’17, Taipei, Taiwan, 2017. ACM.

Sources:
 - https://wuchlei-thu.github.io/
 - https://doi.org/10.1145/3083187.3083210

Notes:
Head tracking dataset with 48 participants (24 male and 24 female) watching 18 spherical videos across five categories. Records head movement, viewing direction and memory recall for each session. Useful for studying user behavior patterns in VR video streaming.
