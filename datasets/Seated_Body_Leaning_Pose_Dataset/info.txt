Name: Seated Body Leaning Pose Dataset
Citation: A. Mavridou, et al. Seated body leaning pose estimation from VR headset data. 2025.

Sources:
 - https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.22134695.v1
 - https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.11466

Notes:
Includes head and hand positions, orientations and gaze vectors during job interview simulations with leaning event labels.

Download Instructions:
Access the Figshare record to download the VR-Data.csv files and leaning annotations for each session (may require registration).
