Name: QoE-Modeling for 360-Degree Videos Dataset
Citation: Wen-Chih Lo, Ching-Ling Fan, Jean Lee, Chun-Ying Huang, Kuan-Ta Chen, and Cheng-Hsin Hsu. 360 Video Viewing Dataset in Head-Mounted Virtual Reality. In Proceedings of the 8th ACM on Multimedia Systems Conference (MMSys), 2017.

Sources:
 - https://github.com/nmsl-nthu/QoE-Modeling-for-360-Degree-Videos-Dataset
 - https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3083187.3083204

Notes:
Contains head orientation logs and QoE ratings for 50 subjects watching ten 360° videos.

Download Instructions:
Clone the GitHub repository and access the orientation_log and rating_log files for each participant.
